ABOUT OUR COMPANY

We are manufacturing and trading group of units and sub-divisions working under one umbrella the 'RAJDEEP GROUP OF INDUSTRIES'. We have started industrial plastic packaging material manufacturing since 13th march 2013 in our Jalgaon unit. We have extended it with other packaging materials like Cellophane tape, Plastic bags and Rolls etc on 29th November 2018.
We are glad to inform that on 1st January 2023, We have entered the global platform of import export, IT solutions and global currency and commodity market.
